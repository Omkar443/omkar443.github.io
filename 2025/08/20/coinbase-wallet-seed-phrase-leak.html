The Coinbase Wallet Recovery Phrase Leak: A Case Study in Bug Bounty Challenges

Published on: August 20, 2025

Introduction

In July 2025, I discovered a critical vulnerability in the Coinbase Wallet for Android that could lead to the irreversible compromise of a user's cryptocurrency assets. What followed was a month-long journey through the bug bounty process that ended not with resolution, but with lessons on the challenges of responsible disclosure.

This post details the vulnerability, the evidence submitted, and the full timeline of interactions.

The Vulnerability: Exposing the Master Key

The recovery phrase (seed phrase) is the master key to a cryptocurrency wallet. Anyone who gains it has permanent control over all funds. Unlike a password, it cannot be rotated.

During testing, I found that Coinbase Wallet displays the recovery phrase after authentication in a standard Android TextView. Unfortunately, this made it visible to the Android Accessibility Framework.

Impact:

Any malicious app with the BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE permission could silently read and exfiltrate the phrase. Accessibility abuse is a well-documented malware vector on Android.

The Evidence: Proof-of-Concept

To demonstrate the risk, I created a working PoC:

  • Functional Android app: Captures the recovery phrase when displayed.
  • Video demo: Shows the phrase being read and exfiltrated.
  • Server-side code: Python script simulating exfiltration to an attacker.
# Simplified proof-of-concept code
class MaliciousAccessibilityService:
  def on_accessibility_event(self, event):
    if self.is_target_app(event):
      seed_phrase = self.extract_text(event)
      self.exfiltrate(seed_phrase)

The results were consistent and reproducible.

Analysis & Lessons

  1. Shifting Goalposts: Closure reasons shifted from "we already fixed it" to "out of scope".
  2. Critical Asset Risk: Recovery phrases are not like passwordsâ€”exposure equals irreversible loss. Security controls must reflect this.
  3. Proof Matters: Video, code, and reproducible PoC strengthened credibility, even if dismissed.
  4. Beyond Bounties: The true value lies in the research, persistence, and contribution to broader security discussions.

Conclusion

This vulnerability demonstrated that Coinbase Wallet's recovery phrase remained exposed despite claimed mitigations. While the bounty program closed the reports, the research underscores a broader issue: crypto wallets must adopt stricter safeguards against accessibility abuse.
